Acquacheta Waterfall



I visited the Acquacheta Waterfall on the 9th day of my Europe trip, on the way to Florence.
I went on a hike to the waterfall, where I was able to try some long exposure shots of moving water.
I also got the opportunity to swim in the river and walk along the ridge of the mountains.
These are the photos which were taken on my Fujifilm XT-1 and a 50-230mm F4.5 lens.
