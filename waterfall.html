Acquacheta waterfall



I to the Acquacheta waterfall on the 9th day of my europe trip on the way to florence
I went on a hike to the waterfall where I could try some long exposure shots of moving water
I also got the opportunity to swim in the river and walk along a the ridge of the mountains
these are the photos which were taken on my Fujifilm xt-1 and a 50-230mm F4.5 lens
