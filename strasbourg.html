Strasbourg



On day 16 of my Europe trip, I visited Strasbourg.
We arrived in the evening and stayed in the small German town of Kehl, taking the tram into Strasbourg.
I found Strasbourg very charming. While there, I enjoyed some flammkuchen,
listened to a brass band performing outside the stunning cathedral,
and watched the sunset along the rivers.
These are the photos taken with my Fujifilm XT-1 and a 50-230mm F4.5 lens.
