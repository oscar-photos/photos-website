Formula 1



I went to London on the 3rd July ahead of the Silverstone Grand Prix
I Went to see The Mclaren event in Trafalgar square where I took a tour of there garage
I saw a few F1 cars ; Senna's MP4/8 (I also saw them run the v12 engine) ;hamilton's mp4-22; Norris's MCL 60
they also had a Formula E car; indy car; and a Hypercar prototype
In piccadilly circus Williams had a popup with Albon's FW45 as well as a few historic cars and trophies
And in Covent garden Aston martin had Stroll's AMR23 right in the street which allowed me to use my Fujifilm to take photos

these are the photos which were taken on my Fujifilm xt-1 and a 50-230mm F4.5 lens and 16-50mm f3.5 lens
