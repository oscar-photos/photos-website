Neuschwanstein Castle



On day 4, I stopped by Neuschwanstein Castle on the way to Lake Achensee.
Neuschwanstein Castle is a 19th-century castle 800 meters up into the Alps and was the inspiration for the castle in Disneyland.
These are the photos which were taken on my Fujifilm XT-1 and a 50-230mm F4.5 lens.
