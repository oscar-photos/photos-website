Zermatt



On day 14, we took the train to Zermatt to see the Matterhorn, which was stunning.
We were fortunate to see it with no clouds. However, I found Zermatt itself to be somewhat dull, as it primarily consisted of super expensive watch shops and felt a bit fabricated to resemble an authentic town.
These are the photos taken with my Fujifilm XT-1 and a 50-230mm F4.5 lens.
