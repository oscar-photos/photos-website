San Marino



I went to San Marino on the 8th day of my Europe trip.
Because San Marino is on a mountain, we had to drive up to a parking lot and then take a 12-story elevator.
When we were in San Marino, we had some nice pizza while overlooking the sea from the top of the mountain.
These are the photos which were taken on my Fujifilm XT-1 and a 50-230mm F4.5 lens.
