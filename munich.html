Munich 2024



I was in Munich for the evening of day 2 and the whole of day 3
I got to go on a tour of the Juristische Bibliothek (law library) see here
I also saw people surfing in Eisbachwelle river see here as well as the regular shots of munich
these are the photos which were taken on my Fujifilm xt-1 and a 50-230mm F4.5 lens







Juristische Bibliothek (law library)



I was got went on a tour of the Juristische Bibliothek in the Justizpalast (town hall)
the room is built in a Art Nouveau which contrasts the rest of the town hall is built in a gothic style
It is the largest room in the town hall and is 3 floors and 30,000 books.
I also saw the magistrate's room before the entrance there was a stained glass window of the statue of liberty









Eisbachwelle (surfing in munich)



In munich there is a place called Eisbachwelle where the the government artificially made a wave for people to surf on
this was my first time trying to photography sport which was fun to experiment with the setting
and i think that a few of the photos are good for my first time
