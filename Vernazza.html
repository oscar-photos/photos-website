Vernazza



I went to Vernazza on the 11th day of my Europe trip.
I visited in the afternoon when it was really hot, and we had to walk down to the town.
While I was there, I swam in the sea, which was crystal clear, and had some gelato.
These are the photos which were taken on my Fujifilm XT-1 and a 50-230mm F4.5 lens.
