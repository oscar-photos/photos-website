Bologna



I stopped in Bologna on the 7th day of my Europe trip, on the way to Cesena.
I was only there for a few short and hot hours.
When I was there, I walked around the center, and then a friendly local told us we could go to San Michele in Bosco to get a good view of the whole city.
These are the photos which were taken on my Fujifilm XT-1 and a 50-230mm F4.5 lens.
